# 首次咨询指南
**Initial Consultation Questions & Script**

---

## 🎯 目标

首次咨询的目的:
1. **评估房源潜力** - 能赚多少钱?
2. **筛选房东** - 房源是否合规?
3. **建立信任** - 展示专业性
4. **达成合作** - 签约!

**时间:** 30-45分钟  
**方式:** 电话、视频或面对面

---

## 📞 开场白

```
Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to chat! I'm Jason.

Before we dive in, I want to make sure this is a good fit for both of us. 
I'm going to ask you some questions about your property and your goals, 
and then I'll share what I can do for you.

Sound good? Great! Let's start.
```

**中文版:**
```
你好 [名字]，感谢你抽时间聊！我是Jason。

在开始之前，我想确保我们双方都适合合作。我会问你一些关于房产和你的目标的问题，
然后我会分享我能为你做什么。

可以吗？太好了！我们开始吧。
```

## 📋 必问问题清单

### 1. 房源基本信息

**Q1: 房子位置和类型?**
- Where is the property located? (Address or neighborhood)
- What type of property? (Single-family / Condo / Apartment / Room)
- How many bedrooms? Bathrooms?
- Approximate square footage?

**记录:**
- Address: _______________
- Type: _______________
- BR: ___ | BA: ___
- Sqft: _______________

**🚩 Red Flags:**
- ❌ ADU (San Jose不允许短租)
- ❌ Sunnyvale (太严格，必须陪同)

---

**Q2: 这是你的主居吗?**
- Do you live in this property as your primary residence?
- How many days per year do you actually stay there?

**为什么重要:**
- San Jose要求主居 (60天/年)
- 如果不是主居 → 不合规，不能合作

**记录:**
- Primary residence? Yes / No
- Days per year: _______________

**🚩 Red Flags:**
- ❌ 不是主居 (违规)
- ❌ 住不满60天/年

---

**Q3: 房子现在是什么状态?**
- Is it currently rented out? (Long-term tenant?)
- Is it vacant?
- Do you use it part-time?

**记录:**
- Current status: Vacant / Long-term rental / Part-time use

**If long-term rental:**
- When does the lease end?
- Are you willing to switch to short-term when lease expires?

---

### 2. 房东目标和动机

**Q4: 为什么考虑Airbnb?**
- What made you interested in short-term rentals?
- What are you hoping to achieve?

**常见答案:**
- "我经常出差，家里空着浪费"
- "我听说短租赚钱更多"
- "我想补贴房贷"

**记录:**
- Motivation: _______________

---

**Q5: 你现在赚多少租金?** (如果在出租)
- How much are you currently earning per month?

**记录:**
- Current income: $_______________/month

**用这个数据对比短租收益!**

---

**Q6: 你的收入目标是多少?**
- Is there a specific monthly income you're hoping to reach?
- What would make this worth it for you?

**记录:**
- Income goal: $_______________/month
- Break-even number: $_______________

---

### 3. 房东的投入意愿

**Q7: 你愿意投入多少时间/精力?**
- How hands-on do you want to be?
- Are you looking for full management or partial?

**常见答案:**
- "我完全不想管，你全包"
- "我想参与一些决策"

**记录:**
- Involvement level: Full hands-off / Some oversight / Collaborative

**调整pitch:**
- Hands-off → 推25% full-service package
- Wants oversight → 推20% basic package

---

**Q8: 你愿意投资升级房源吗?**
- Are you willing to make improvements if needed?
  - Smart lock? ($150-250)
  - Better WiFi? ($50-100)
  - Furniture/decor? (varies)

**记录:**
- Willing to invest? Yes / No / Maybe
- Budget: $_______________

---

### 4. 合规性检查

**Q9: 你有business license吗?**
- Do you have a San Jose business license?
- If not, are you willing to get one? (~$195/year)

**记录:**
- Has license? Yes / No
- Willing to get? Yes / No

**如果不愿意 → 不合作**

---

**Q10: 你了解San Jose的短租规定吗?**
- Are you familiar with San Jose's short-term rental regulations?

**简单解释:**
```
"San Jose requires:
- Property must be your primary residence
- You need a business license (~$195/year)
- 10% TOT tax (Airbnb collects automatically)
- Maximum 180 nights per year if you're not home
- I'll help you stay compliant with all of this."
```

**记录:**
- Aware of regulations? Yes / No

---

### 5. 时间线和期望

**Q11: 你想什么时候开始?**
- When are you hoping to get started?
- Is there a specific date you want the listing live?

**记录:**
- Target start date: _______________

**Set realistic expectations:**
```
"Great! Typically it takes 1-2 weeks to:
- Get photos taken
- Create the listing
- Get your business license
- Set up the smart lock

We can have you live and taking bookings by [Date]."
```

---

**Q12: 你对Airbnb有什么顾虑吗?**
- What are your biggest concerns about doing Airbnb?

**常见顾虑 + 回应:**

**"陌生人来我家"**
> "Totally understandable. Airbnb has $3M insurance, and I screen all guests. 
> Plus, you can start with just 1-2 bookings to test the waters."

**"太麻烦"**
> "That's exactly why I'm here! You literally don't have to do anything. 
> I handle 100% of the work — guests, cleaning, emergencies, everything."

**"怕损坏"**
> "I do before/after inspections, and Airbnb covers up to $3M in damage. 
> In my experience, 99% of guests are respectful. And if something breaks, 
> I file the claim for you."

**"邻居投诉"**
> "I enforce strict house rules (no parties, quiet hours). Most neighbors 
> won't even know it's a rental. And I'm the local contact, so I handle 
> any issues immediately."

**记录:**
- Concerns: _______________

## 💰 展示价值 - 收益估算

**Now let's talk numbers!**

### 使用AirDNA/Airbtics数据

**提前查好周边竞品:**
- Similar properties in [neighborhood]
- Average nightly rate: $_____ 
- Average occupancy: _____%
- Monthly revenue: $_____

**公式:**
```
Conservative estimate:
Nightly rate × 15 nights/month = $_____ gross income
- Cleaning (2x/month @ $100): -$200
- Airbnb service fee (~14%): -$_____
= $_____ net income

Your share (75%): $_____/month
My share (25%): $_____/month
```

**对比长租:**
```
Current long-term rent: $1,500/month
Estimated short-term (you keep): $2,800/month
Difference: +$1,300/month (+87% increase!)
```

**Script:**
```
"Based on similar properties in [neighborhood], you could realistically 
earn around $[X] per month after expenses. That's about [X]% more than 
what you're making now.

And that's a conservative estimate. In peak season, it could be even higher."
```

## ⚠️ 筛选标准 - 什么时候说No

**不要合作如果:**

❌ **不合规**
- 不是主居
- 住不满60天/年
- 是ADU
- 在Sunnyvale (要求陪同)

❌ **不现实的期望**
- "我要赚$10k/month" (1BR in average neighborhood)
- "我不想花一分钱" (连smart lock都不愿买)

❌ **红旗房东**
- 不愿意签合同
- 要求50%+分成给你 (不合理)
- 态度恶劣/不尊重

**礼貌拒绝:**
```
"I appreciate you reaching out. Unfortunately, based on [reason], 
I don't think I'd be the best fit for your property.

I'd recommend [alternative solution, e.g., checking Airbnb's self-management 
resources / hiring a local property manager / etc.].

Best of luck!"
```
