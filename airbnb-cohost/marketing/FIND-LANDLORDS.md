## 🎤 电话话术 (首次通话)

### Opening
```
Hi [Name], this is Jason. Thanks for taking my call!

I help homeowners in San Jose turn their properties into profitable Airbnbs 
without any work on their part. 

Do you have a couple minutes to chat about your property at [address]?
```

### 发现需求 (问问题)
```
1. "Are you currently renting it out long-term, or is it sitting empty?"
2. "How much are you earning now?" (如果长租)
3. "Have you ever considered short-term rentals?"
4. "What's your biggest concern about Airbnb?" (解决objection)
```

### 展示价值
```
"Based on similar properties in [neighborhood], you could earn around 
$3,200/month with Airbnb, compared to $1,500 long-term rent.

I handle everything:
- Listing creation
- All guest communication
- Cleaning coordination
- Compliance (business license, taxes, etc.)

You literally do nothing except collect higher rent checks."
```

### 解决Objections

**"听起来麻烦"**
> "That's exactly why I exist! You don't lift a finger. I manage everything 
> 24/7. You'll actually have LESS work than a long-term tenant."

**"我不想陌生人来我家"**
> "Totally understandable. Airbnb has $3M insurance, and I screen all guests. 
> Plus, we can start with just 1-2 bookings to test it out."

**"合法吗?"**
> "Absolutely. San Jose allows short-term rentals as long as it's your primary 
> residence and you have a business license. I help you get compliant."

**"我不懂Airbnb"**
> "Perfect — that's my job! You don't need to know anything. I set it all up."

### Closing
```
"How about this: I'll create a free revenue estimate for your property. 
No commitment, just numbers. If it makes sense, we can move forward. 
If not, no worries.

Can I come by this week to take some photos and measurements?"
```
