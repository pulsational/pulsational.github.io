Frequently Asked Questions

Is PiggyLearn a real bank?

No, PiggyLearn is a financial education app that simulates a banking experience. No real money is involved. All balances and transactions are for learning purposes only.

How do I add my child to our family bank?

Go to your Bank Dashboard ‚Üí Bank Members ‚Üí "Add Child Member". You can create a simple family code and PIN for your child to log in without needing an email address.

How do I set up automatic allowances?

From your Bank Dashboard ‚Üí "Scheduled Payments" ‚Üí "+ New Payment". You can set up weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly recurring transfers to your children's accounts.

What's included in Premium?

Premium includes: up to 10 family members (vs 2 free), GIC investments with guaranteed returns, advanced scheduled payments, and priority support.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID or Google Play account. Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription on your device to manage or cancel.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. If you're a bank owner, the bank will be transferred or deleted.

Is my data safe?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. We don't sell your data, and you can delete your account at any time. See our Privacy Policy for details.
