# Welcome to Banff/Jasper Anshul! ## Overview The word "Banff" covers several regions, including several national parks such as Baff, Jasper, Glacie, Yoho etc. I will name the most attracting elements as lakes, mountains and animals. When planning a trip, especially with parents, keep in mind: don't stuff too many things in one day. The attractions are distant and driving in mountains requires more patient. ## Planning You'll need to plan for several things ahead as banff is a bit special. ### Travelling Flight to Calgary and get a car rental please. If you have 4 adults, better go for an SUV. There are shuttle buses in Banff town but they wont reach anywhere you want to go. **KEEP IN MIND**: - the Lake Moraine and Lake Louise no longer accept personal vehicles anymore thus you will have to buy tickets. Official website [here](https://parks.canada.ca/pn-np/ab/banff/visit/parkbus/louise) but you will have other private run companies with slightly more expensive options. - There will be a lot of driving around. Such as from Banff town to Lake Louise will cost you about 40-50 minutes without traffic. Save enough time when making plans. - Be careful with your gas level. If driving for long distance such as from Banff town to Jasper town, always check for the next gas station. ### Living Try Airbnb/booking or google map such as [this](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Banff+Park+Lodge+Resort+Hotel+%26+Conference+Centre/@51.181657,-115.5741415,15.55z/data=!4m9!3m8!1s0x5370ca45b47cd2c1:0xcd8d91eb39607182!5m2!4m1!1i2!8m2!3d51.17757!4d-115.57425!16s%2Fg%2F1vq9lzdy?entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI1MDQyNy4xIKXMDSoJLDEwMjExNDUzSAFQAw%3D%3D). Highly recommand to live in Cammore instead of Banff, because Banff town is pretty expensive while Cammore is a lot cheaper and not too far away from Banff town. ### Cloth Rocky Mountains can be cold. Dress normally but be prepared for snowing. I suggest to have extra coats or jackets for everyone in your car. ### Food Don't feed wild animals! Have enough food in your car is very important. What we usually do is once we got our rental car, we will go to Costco and grab bottle waters and instant noodles. Our special trick, as Chinese we will also go to T&T and get a lot of self-heating hot-pots such as [this](https://www.tntsupermarket.com/chs/52643901-tianxiaohua-spicy-vege-hotpot.html). This only requires cold water and you will have a nice and hot meal anywhere in the wild. Other than fast foods, you can find cafes in the populated area such as Banff town and Canmmore. Actually the food in Banff town is amazing and you should spend a full day in Banff town and more if you want to. There are also many places you can find food such as [Jasper National Park Icefield Information Centre and Glacier Gallery](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Jasper+National+Park+Icefield+Information+Centre+and+Glacier+Gallery/@52.0803759,-117.3202663,10.51z/data=!3m1!5s0x5377ff21b355f697:0x247b798bcdf1a68b!4m6!3m5!1s0x5377ff21c9fe5a93:0x4d1bb0a9a45a4538!8m2!3d52.2203483!4d-117.2242371!16s%2Fg%2F12jrgjj4b?entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI1MDQyNy4xIKXMDSoJLDEwMjExNDUzSAFQAw%3D%3D). Dont worry if you did not prepare enough food, but with your loved food can for sure make this trip better! ### Animal You may encounter wild animals anytime. Most common ones are elks and goats. Bears are not so rare either. Goats/sheeps sometimes stop in the road and blocked the traffic. Do not horn. Tip: if you see a lot of cars parking on the shoulder, in a middle of no where, most probably they are stopping car for animals. ### Duration Suggest to save 5-7 days excluding flight time. Longer the better. Every day, plan for 2-3 sites is the best, depending on your preference. Examples for a typical day: - Lake louise and Lake moraine. - Commute between Banff and Jasper, plus one attraction such as stop at the icefield walk ## Plances My personal very biased interesting places! ### Driving Yes. Driving itself is enjoyable. You can stop anywhere you like. Don't rush. ### The famouse 2 lakes: Louise and Moraine. Shuttle bus required.
