### Work task 5: Deep Research — Real Pastor Communication
#### Overview
Researched how real pastors/priests conduct spiritual conversations, covering 4 areas.

#### Work log
Key findings applied to prompts:
1. **Confession**: priests are mostly silent, speak only to counsel briefly + absolve. Our AI talks too much.
2. **Intercession**: pastors always ask before praying, then shift to "speaking to God" voice
3. **Sermons**: personal vulnerability first ("I struggled too"), then Scripture
4. **Pastoral counseling**: never lead with Scripture during acute crisis; CPE-trained chaplains use reflective listening
