Troubleshooting

App is slow or unresponsive

  • Close the app completely (swipe up from the app switcher) and reopen it
  • Make sure your iPhone has at least 1 GB of free storage
  • Restart your iPhone if the issue persists

AI responses seem incomplete or cut off

  • The on-device model has a context window limit. For longer conversations, start a new prayer session
  • Close other memory-intensive apps to give Absolvo more resources

Face ID is not working for Confession

  • Make sure Face ID is enabled in your iPhone's Settings > Face ID & Passcode
  • In Absolvo, go to Settings and check that the Face ID toggle is enabled under Privacy
  • If your device does not support Face ID or Touch ID, the biometric lock will not be available
